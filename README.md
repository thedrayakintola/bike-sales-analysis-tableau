**Bike Sales Analysis Dashboard**
**Overview**

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard developed to analyze bike sales performance across multiple business dimensions.

The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profitability, customer demographics, geographic distribution, and product performance to support business decision-making.

**Objectives**

The objectives of this project were to:

Analyze sales performance
Identify high-performing products
Evaluate sales by gender
Examine customer age groups
Explore geographic sales distribution
Monitor yearly business performance

Year

**Business Insights**
Some key findings include:

Bikes generated the largest proportion of revenue.
Road Bikes and Mountain Bikes were the highest-selling product categories.
Sales performance varied across countries.
Young adults and adults represented the strongest customer segments.
The dashboard allows interactive filtering for year and geography.
